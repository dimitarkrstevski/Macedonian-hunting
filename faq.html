FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

You can fly by a plane to the airport Alexander the Great that is 10km from Skopje and there are flights from almost every city in Europe at least 2 times per week.
Another option is to fly through Thessaloniki where there are daily flights to all major cities in Europe as well as overseas flights. The road from Thessaloniki to our hunting grounds is about 160 km or 2 hour drive by car where one of our employees will wait for you and will bring you to the agreed location.
If you are coming with a car from Germany, Austria, Switzerland, Italy and other countries the road is via “The Brotherhood and Unity” highway directly from Ljubljana to Skopje.
If you come from any part of Italy you can use a ferry which departs at night of any port in Italy and arrives in the morning to the port of Igoumenitsa in neighboring Greece where you have 6 hours drive by car to our hunting grounds.
If you choose any of the options above, you will receive precise routes from us so we can easily meet

You only need to bring with you your travel document- passport or ID card. The license for the weapons is obligated in both cases- if you bring your weapons with you and if you rent from us.

Yes, you can come and hunt alone and if you want we can involve you in another group when we make group hunting.

Of course you can carry with you several types of guns depending on the needs and the type of hunt you have chosen. Certainly you will need to carry your hunting licenses for all weapons individually or together in a single document, depending on which country you come from.

Yes, you can rent guns from us. We have several different types of weapons available from shotguns at 12 and 20 caliber as submachine guns or over and under. If you hunt big game we have hunting carbines- rifles in various kinds of gauges.

Yes, you can change your package depending on availability. If nothing suits you, we can keep your reservation for next season.

Yes, you can combine different packages, including **small and big game** hunts.

Yes, you can cancel **30 days before** the hunt to allow us time to replace your spot.

You can pay **via bank transfer** or by **credit card** on our website.

Each hunter can bring up to **5 dogs**. If you have **more than 5**, you will need additional people in your vehicle.

Your dog(s) need a **passport**, a **regular vaccine**, and a **rabies vaccine**.

Yes, we provide an **official certificate** for small and big game that allows export. We can also prepare taxidermy and ship trophies internationally.
