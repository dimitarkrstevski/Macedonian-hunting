WILD BOAR

P R I C E

Hunting Information

  • • Hunting season:
    From 01 October to 31 January in a free range (all year in a fenced area)
  • • Hunting types:
    By stalking for trophy hunt or driven hunt in a free range or fenced area. (Hunting Wild Boar can be mixed with hunting wild goat, red deer, fallow deer, roe deer in a free range or fenced area)
  • • Hunters:
    Minimum from 3 hunters to a group of 15 hunters maximum
  • • Hunting areas:
    Mountains, hills, valley
  • • Trophy:
    The trophy can be paid lump sum or according to its size measured in cm. Usually, the trophies are between 14-22 cm and more.
  • • Limits:
    No limits per hunter per day

Other Conditions

  • Wounded and not found Wild Boar is paid 50% of the trophy value requested by the hunter.
  • Rates and Accommodation:
    • 4 nights / 3 days hunting
    • 5 nights / 4 days hunting
    • Additional hunting day is on your request
  • Documents Required for the Hunter:
    • Valid passport or identity card
    • Hunting license from your country
    • Permit for export guns
    • Passport with rabies vaccination for the dog
  • The Rates includes:
    • Transfer from the airport or any border pass and back
    • Accommodation in a hotel or a hunting lodge in double rooms on a full board basis
    • Transfer from hotel to the hunting area
    • Complete hunting organization
    • Professional guides
    • Hunting license valid for all period
    • First trophy preparation
    • Export documents for the trophy
  • The Rates does not include:
    • Transport to/from Macedonia
    • Renting hunting gun 50 € per day
    • Box of 25 cartridges 10 €
    • Renting hunting rifle 100 € per day
    • Box of 20 bullets 60 €
    • Renting hunting dog 25 € per day
    • Single room 15 € per night
    • Personal insurance
