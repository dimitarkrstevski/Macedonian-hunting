About Us

Macedonia is a landlocked state in the heart of the Balkan Peninsula in Southeast Europe. With a total area of 25,713 km2, Macedonia is bordered by Serbia to the north, Bulgaria to the east, Greece to the south, and Albania to the west. It is a mountainous country with flat area and agricultural land. The climate here is transitional from Mediterranean to continental. The average annual temperature is 11,5 ° C. The warmest month is July, with an average temperature of 22,2 ° C, while the coldest month is January with an average temperature of 0,3 ° C. Мost of the territory of Macedonia is with an altitude of 500 to 1,000 meters, but there can be found mountain top up to 2.700m asl. The Central European Time Zone is UTC+01:00

Hunting Association Tikvesija is a company specialized in organizing hunting trips in Macedonia. We have been in the field of hunting since 2011 and with our own 60.000 Ha (hectares) of hunting terrains. During all of these years, as concessioners, we work with selected groups and individual hunters depending on what type of game you want to hunt, big or small game.

On our terrains can be hunted from small game: quails, turtle doves, wood pigeons, pheasants, ducks, woodcocks, gray partridges, rock partridges.

From big game: roe deer, fallow deer, red deer, wild boar, mouflon, wild goat, feral goat, kri kri-ibex, racka sheep, barbary sheep, wolf.

As a curiosity fact, Macedonia is one of the rare places in the world with the biggest percent of wolf trophy realization which is guarantee in a few days. All of the type of big or small game abovementioned can be hunted in a free range or fenced area.

Our association owns the best hunting reserves in Macedonia, so we will offer you a complete professional service to respond at most to your wishes. From the arriving to the return home, your hunting journey will be guided by our friendly and qualified team. We have a skilled and proficient PH who will guide across hunting terrains during your whole stay. All of our employees are professional hunters, specialized for both big and small game. With our team, over the years, we have had the pleasure to work with hundreds of hunters all over the world, and these experiences resulted with lifelong friendships with many of our clients.
